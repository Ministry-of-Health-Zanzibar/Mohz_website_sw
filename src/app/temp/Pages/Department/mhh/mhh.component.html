Mnazi Mmoja Hospital (MMH)

MMH serves as the only tertiary and referral hospital in Zanzibar, providing comprehensive healthcare services through its multiple campuses and departments.

Overview

MMH is a semi-autonomous public hospital established by Act No 3 of 2016. It has a bed capacity of 678 spread across three campuses:

  • Main Campus: Located in downtown Stone Town with 541 beds.
  • Mwembeladu Maternity Home: Located within Stone Town, has 37 beds.
  • Kidongo Chekundu Mental Hospital: Located outside the downtown area, has 100 beds.

Despite being a tertiary institution, MMH also manages outpatient cases, making it one of the busiest hospitals in Zanzibar. In 2016, MMH served 117,263 outpatients and 34,767 inpatients, with 5,033 admissions and 608 deaths reported.

  • Operations & Administration
