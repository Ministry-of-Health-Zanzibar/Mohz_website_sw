Medical Store - Zanzibar

Medical Store is one of the institutions of the Ministry of Health Zanzibar responsible for promoting health care delivery by ensuring access to essential medicines and quality medical supplies. Before 2011, it was a unit under the Chief Pharmacist. In 2011, it was upgraded to a Department with warehouses in Maruhubi (Unguja) and Micheweni (Pemba).

Currently, the Medical Store handles the receipt, storage, and distribution of essential drugs, medical devices, diagnostic equipment, and provides various information to help the Government make informed decisions.

Medical Store Divisions

  • Customer Service Division: Ensures customer satisfaction and handles inquiries.
  • Quality Control Division (Quality Assurance): Ensures the quality of medicines and supplies.
  • Procurement and Medication Management Division: Manages the procurement of medicines and medical supplies.
  • Division of Administration and Finance: Manages the financial operations and administration of the Medical Store.

Services Provided by Medical Store

  • Receiving Medicines and Medical Supplies: The Medical Store is responsible for receiving medicines and supplies purchased by the government and partners.
  • Storage and Handling: It supervises the proper storage and handling of medicines and supplies.
  • Distribution: Distribution of medicines and supplies to all health centers and hospitals in Unguja and Pemba, currently serving 190 government and defense centers.
  • Supervision: It supervises the practice of medicine and supplies in health centers and hospitals.
  • Training and Guidance: Provides training and guidance to pharmacists and storekeepers in health centers and hospitals regarding proper storage and handling.
